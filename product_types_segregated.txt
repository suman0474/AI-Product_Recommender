PRODUCT TYPES SEGREGATION
======================================================================

INSTRUMENTS
--------------------
- Ball Valve: Controls fluid flow by rotating a perforated ball.
- Turbidity Sensor: Measures the cloudiness or haziness of a fluid.
- Temperature Calibrator: Generates precise temperature signals to test and calibrate temperature instruments.
- Control Valve: Regulates fluid flow rate in a process control system.
- Oxygen Sensor: Measures the concentration of oxygen in a gas or liquid.
- Thermocouple Assembly: Measures temperature based on the voltage generated by two dissimilar metals.
- Toc Analyzer: Measures the total organic carbon content in water.
- Tachometer: Measures the rotational speed of a shaft or disk.
- Remote Unit: A distributed I/O or control module located away from the main controller.
- Temperature Transmitter: Converts a temperature sensor signal into a standardized output signal.
- Alarm System: Notifies operators of abnormal process conditions.
- Emission Sensor: Detects and measures pollutants in exhaust gases.
- Hmi System: Provides a graphical interface for operators to interact with a control system.
- Flow Meter: Measures the volumetric or mass flow rate of a fluid.
- Moisture Sensor: Measures the water content in a substance or environment.
- Temperature Sensor: Detects and measures temperature.
- Energy Meter: Measures electrical energy consumption.
- Limit Switch: Detects the presence or absence of an object or the end of a mechanical travel.
- Flow Controller: Regulates and maintains a desired fluid flow rate.
- Voltage Sensor: Measures electrical voltage.
- Refractometer: Measures the refractive index of a substance, often to determine concentration.
- Flame Sensor: Detects the presence of a flame.
- Pressure Gauge: Displays the pressure of a fluid or gas.
- Thickness Sensor: Measures the thickness of materials.
- Orp Sensor: Measures the oxidation-reduction potential of a liquid.
- Controller: Receives input signals, processes them, and generates output signals to control a process.
- Shutdown System: Initiates an emergency shutdown sequence in critical situations.
- Level Transmitter: Measures and transmits the level of a substance in a tank or vessel.
- Damper: Regulates airflow in ducts or flues.
- Linear Encoder: Measures linear position or displacement.
- Actuator: Converts an energy source into mechanical motion to operate a valve or damper.
- Temperature Indicator: Displays the measured temperature.
- Temperature Controller: Regulates and maintains a desired temperature.
- Ph Sensor: Measures the pH (acidity or alkalinity) of a liquid.
- Humidity Sensor: Measures the relative humidity of air or gas.
- Current Sensor: Measures electrical current.
- Laser Displacement Sensor: Measures displacement or distance using a laser beam.
- Proximity Sensor: Detects the presence of an object without physical contact.
- Leak Sensor: Detects the presence of leaks (e.g., gas, liquid).
- Safety Valve: Automatically relieves excess pressure to prevent system damage.
- Smoke Sensor: Detects the presence of smoke.
- Gas Analyzer: Measures the concentration of specific gases in a mixture.
- Signal Transmitter: Converts a sensor signal into a standardized output signal for transmission.
- Smart Transmitter: A transmitter with advanced diagnostic and communication capabilities.
- Light Curtain: Creates a protective optical barrier to detect objects or personnel entering a hazardous area.
- Displacement Sensor: Measures the linear or angular position change of an object.
- Rotary Encoder: Measures angular position or rotational speed.
- Scada System: A supervisory control and data acquisition system for monitoring and controlling large-scale processes.
- Vibration Sensor: Measures the vibration levels of machinery.
- Process Control System: An integrated system that manages and automates industrial processes.
- Particulate Sensor: Measures the concentration of solid particles in a gas or liquid.
- Logger: Records data over time for analysis.
- Ultrasonic Sensor: Measures distance or detects objects using ultrasonic sound waves.
- Strain Gauge: Measures strain (deformation) on an object.
- Viscosity Sensor: Measures the resistance of a fluid to flow.
- Gas Sensor: Detects and measures the concentration of specific gases.
- Flow Transmitter: Measures and transmits the flow rate of a fluid.
- Pressure Sensor: Detects and measures pressure.
- Density Sensor: Measures the mass per unit volume of a substance.
- Sound Sensor: Detects and measures sound levels.
- Gateway: Connects different communication networks or protocols.
- Conductivity Sensor: Measures the electrical conductivity of a liquid.
- Pressure Transmitter: Measures and transmits the pressure of a fluid or gas.
- Water Sensor: Detects the presence of water or measures its level/quality.

ACCESSORIES
--------------------
- Power Supply: Converts AC power to DC power to energize instruments.
- Flow Element: A primary device (e.g., orifice plate) used with a differential pressure transmitter to measure flow.
- Cable Glands And Conduit Fittings: Secure and seal cables at enclosure entry points and connect conduits.
- Amplifier: Boosts the strength of an electrical signal.
- Instrument Cable: Specialized cable for connecting industrial instruments and transmitting signals.
- Signal Isolator: Electrically separates input and output signals to prevent ground loops and noise.
- Surge Protector: Protects electronic equipment from voltage spikes.
